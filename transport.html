Transportation system


The majority of vehicles on the road could be self-driving by 2050, according to enthusiasts for electric and autonomous cars. They believe technology, plus the push for cleaner air will be the incentive for big changes in the way we get from A to B.


Transportation system will be developed in following ways:-

•Flying cars will exist in the coming years.

•Super tall buildings will serve as spaceports.

•To increase speed, airplanes might not have windows.

•People will be driven around in driverless pods

•Point-to-Point Suborbital Flights

•Hyperloops and High-speed Trains

•You'll be able to ride a Hyperloop.

•It will be possible to ride in a hypersonic jet high in the atmosphere
